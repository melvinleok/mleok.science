Kepler Orbit: Geometric Integration

This interactive demonstration highlights the difference between standard numerical methods and geometric integrators. Watch how explicit Euler rapidly gains energy, 4th order Runge-Kutta slowly drifts in energy, while symplectic Euler strictly bounds the energy error, preserving the orbit indefinitely.
