Geometric Dynamics on a Manifold

Simulating a particle constrained to the surface of a sphere. Standard methods (EE, RK4) integrate the analytical equations but fail to perfectly enforce the geometric manifold, eventually causing the particle to drift off the sphere. Geometric methods (RATTLE) perfectly preserve the constraint $|q|=1$ and bound the system energy.
